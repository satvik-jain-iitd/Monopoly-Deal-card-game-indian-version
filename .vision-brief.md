## Architectural Notes

- Game engine uses a PURE REDUCER pattern — no side effects in cases
- All randomness (shuffle/deal) happens before dispatch
- Multiplayer has two modes: Cloud (WebSocket via Cloudflare Worker) + Offline/P2P (WebRTC QR exchange)
- RTC_CONFIG in `useWebRTC.js` has `iceServers: []` — **no STUN/TURN**
- JSN chain only exists for Rent collection — NOT for Deal Breaker or other targeted actions
- Pass-and-play hides hand via `PassDeviceModal` between turns
