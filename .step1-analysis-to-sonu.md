## Sprint Brief — 5 Bugs

### Bug 1: Online Multiplayer Communication Failure
- Host creates room → stuck on "waiting for players"
- Likely: no STUN/TURN servers in ICE config, SDP stripping only keeps 'host' candidates
- Files: `src/multiplayer/useWebRTC.js`, `src/multiplayer/rtcUtils.js`, `src/multiplayer/useMultiplayer.js`

### Bug 2: Wild Card Colour Lock (Pass & Play)
- Dual-colour wild assigned at play time → can never change colour later
- Real rule: "You may change the colour of your Wild Property cards at any time on your turn"
- Files: `src/game/gameLogic.js` (playPropertyCard), `src/game/useGameState.js`, `src/components/screens/GameScreen.jsx`

### Bug 3: Missing JSN Chain for Deal Breaker
- Deal Breaker instantly steals set, never prompts target for Just Say No
- JSN works for Rent but not Deal Breaker
- Files: `src/game/useGameState.js` (DEAL_BREAKER_STEAL case), `src/components/game/ActionModal.jsx`

### Bug 4: Rent Payment – Hidden Hand
- Payment modal shows bank + properties + buildings but NOT the payer's hand
- Player can't see what they have when deciding what to pay
- Files: `src/components/game/ActionModal.jsx` (PaymentSheet)

### Bug 5: Rent Card with Zero Matching Properties
- Game allows playing a Rent card even when player owns no matching colour
- Should block "Play as Action" when no matching property exists
- Files: `src/components/screens/GameScreen.jsx` (RentSelector + PlayOptions)

## Codebase Summary
- React 19 + Vite 8 + MUI v9
- Pure reducer game engine (`useGameState.js`)
- WebSocket + WebRTC for multiplayer
- Pass-and-play core mode
